How can you help?

We have three different departments where you can become a volunteer. Which one you choose is up to you, we are happy to get help in any of them! You don't need to have any previous knowledge in the department you are volunteering in. We will take care of you and teach you as the time goes.

Second hand

In our Second hand store in Kalmar, we present a wide range of products with everything from furtinures to jewleries. Among some thing you get to do is to sell furtinures, be a cashier or serve our famous shrimp sandwich in our café.

Ateljén

In Ateljén, we value creative ideas. From textiles which has been donated to us, we transform them to useful things. A curtain can be a dress, a blanket can be a coat. If you want to embrace the creative part of you, this is the right choice!

Handtaget

If you are a handy person Handtaget may be the right place for you. We have a lot of different projects going on. It can be to build a table, renovate a toilet or clean out a storage. We also make unique jewelry which we sell to raise money to people in need.
