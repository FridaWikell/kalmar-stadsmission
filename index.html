Who are we?

Kalmar Stadsmission is a non-profit organisation which helps people in need. We work in Kalmar and its surroundings with people who due to abuse, health or social problems have ended up in a vulnerable life situation.

What do we do?

We do a lot of different things. We help people get back to work. We serve breakfast. We give away groceries so families can have food on the table. We give people shelter. But most important of all, we see everybody and we give everybody a second chance.
